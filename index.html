All Oscars, All the Time

Best Picture Oscar Winners of the 21st Century, Ranked

From Gladiator  to One Battle After Another — our rankings of every film to take home the top Oscar since the year 2000, from worst to best

By

The Oscar is the highest award for a film. But not always the film that won the Oscar is the best film. Yes, there are films that have become legends and you want to watch them again, but there are films that you watched once and forgot, and it would be better if you didn't watch them at all. I have watched all the films that have won the Oscar in this millennium, except for Spotlight, Chicago, Argo, The King's Speech, and A Beautiful Mind (but I will correct this), and I have arranged them from worst to best.
